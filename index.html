Who is behind this project?

Hi, im Pixltoshi and im just trying to have some fun with ordinals.

What's the collection about?

It's an introduction to recursion for most and for the rest just a way to get involved with ordinals. The community won't be large but hopefully active and willing to build stuff together. I have a love for Bitcoin and a long history with NFTs so I wanted to do something with both of them. If you don't know about recursion hopefully this experience helps you understand just a little better.

How big is the collection?

This is a genesis collection so there will only be 100 in total. You'll have over 150 traits to choose from while building your pal so the possibilities for these are practically endless.

Is every build unique?

Yes, each and every pal will be unique and have no clones. There are some scripts running in the background to make sure that no duplicates are created. That extends to the same pfp with a different background which should not be possible, I hope.

How much do they cost?

These are "free" if you consider paying the fees to inscribe an ordinal free. It shouldn't cost anymore than a few dollars to inscribe your creation.

How It Works

1. Build Your Pixlpal
We created a fun little builder for you to create your own pfp. Just select different traits from the category tabs above until your happy with your pal. You can see a visual representation of the pfp before inscribing in the image container above.

2. Compile Your Traits
This is where the magic of recursion comes in. Each trait represents a unique identifier which allows you to piece together your ordinal using an SVG file. Once you are finished building your Pixlpal you can press the "compile" button on the bottom right. This will open a new window where you can see the SVG code that has been created and download it. You will need to download this SVG file in order to inscribe (mint) your ordinal.

3. Inscribe Your Ordinal
Now that you have your SVG file you are ready to inscribe. This part is simple, just click on the "inscribe" button located below the SVG string that is generated for you and then drag that file into the upload field in unisat. This page will load on the page you need to upload the file to. You will need to pay a small fee (only a few $$) to inscribe and then you are all done. You should see the ordinal in your wallet after a short period of time.
